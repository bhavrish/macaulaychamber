About Us


Macaulay Chamber is a chamber music ensemble at CUNY Macaulay Honors College. We are a performance-based club and have performed at locations across NYC including nursing homes, performance halls, and of course, the central Macaulay building by Central Park. Our club is comprised of musicians playing all genres of music from classical to pop to even jazz. We welcome all instruments even if they are not necessarily used in tradiational chamber music ensembles. For further questions, please email macaulaychamber@gmail.com.
